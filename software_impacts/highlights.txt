DaCaF: Bayes-optimal per-metric inference for probabilistic multi-label classification

- DaCaF returns the exact Bayes-optimal prediction for a chosen MLC metric
- Model-agnostic drop-in layer on top of any probabilistic model P(y|x)
- Seven per-metric rules verified equal to brute-force enumeration
- Covers two whole families of MLC metrics in a single tool
- Diagnostic flags metrics whose Bayes-optimal prediction is trivial
